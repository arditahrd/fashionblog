The Best Street Style Photos From Taipei Fashion Week Spring 2023

In Taipei, showgoers are embracing lowrise silhouettes and logos. We’re also seeing an emphasis on accessories and jewelry with bold hair clips and couture-esque baubles making the rounds. Follow along all week long as our photographers capture the best street style photos from the week.

Bella Hadid’s Street Style Guide to the Best Fall

Bella Hadid is not only one of the biggest names on the runway, but street style-wise, the supermodel is watched just as closely, if not more. The Bella Hadid street style approach to dressing often includes a mix of Y2K-inspired fashion, like low-rise trousers and baggy jeans with the latest athletic attire from Balenciaga and Adidas x Wales Bonner. Her love of vintage collectibles from Jean Paul Gautier, Marc Jacobs, Chanel, and more is no secret either. When it comes to her more fall-forward outfits, Hadid continues to deliver, relying on heavy-hitting fall wardrobe essentials like timeless trench coats, lived-in denim, bomber jackets, and more.

The Runway Debut of the Archival Looks That Inspired the Kim x Dolce & Gabbana Collection

Some years ago, Kim Kardashian explained at a press conference in Milan that she and her stylist had made a Dolce & Gabbana dream list, comprising dozens of archival looks from the brand that Kim would then acquire whenever she found them available. She pulled from that ever-growing collection when dressing for her sister Kourtney’s wedding in Portofino, and that set off what grew into the Kim x Dolce & Gabbana spring 2023 collection, based on D&G pieces from 1987 to 2007.

Another person opting for fun suits was Tracee Ellis Ross.
